IIS Technical Training

The Immunization Information Systems Support Branch (IISSB) of CDC is looking to host an in person training meeting for software developers and other technical experts who create, support and maintain IIS HL7 interfaces. This one day training meeting is expected to cover the following material:

  • Review of the standards defined in the CDC HL7 Implementation Guide.
  • How to implement interfaces that are compatible with the latest national standards.
  • How to implement interfaces that will be forwards compatible with future standards.
  • Information presented will focus on current Meaningful Use version 2 standards for VXU messages.
  • Preview and technical information about new standards that are coming.

Location

Looking to host in person training in Chicago. This is centrally located to reduce travel time for project participants.

Date

Looking at the first week in April.

Travel Costs

IISSB is looking to cover the cost of travel to participants. Space is limited so IISSB will prioritize space in order to involve representatives from as many technical teams as possible. Please RSVP your interest in attending as soon as possible in order to be considered.
