Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a domain name?
Try looking at register.com. Type in the domain name you wish to buy and register.com will tell you if it is taken yet. If it isn't you will be able to purchase it right there. Registration costs 70 USD for the first 2 years and 35 USD for every year after that. If the domain name is already taken register.com will even suggest other possible names.
