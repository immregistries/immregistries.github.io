Introduction

I am a software developer with expertise in Java and HL7. For seven years I have worked with immunization registries to connect them with immunization provider systems. It is my privilege to work with hundreds of dedicated health and IT professionals across the country whose work makes a real difference in the lives of people. My job is to improve public health ... I love my job!

Reading Recommendations

Getting Things Done
This book was recommended to me last December. I read it and immediately started implementing my own personal GTD system. It has significantly improved my productivity and decreased my stress. I would highly recommend this to anyone working in Public Health.

Upgrade Your Life: The Lifehacker guide to working smarter, faster, better
This book is a how-to-guide for those of us who work primarily in the Internet world. It gives handy tricks to get most work things done quickly and avoid time wasting activities. It also references Getting Things Done and gives examples of how to make your system digital.
